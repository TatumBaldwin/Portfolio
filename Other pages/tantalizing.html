Tantalizing Designs - Business Package

"I embarked on the creation of Tantalizing Designs, with an abundance of ideas buzzing in my mind. The question "who am I?" must have crossed my mind a million times, as I consider myself an artist, a problem solver, a hard worker, and a nature and animal enthusiast, among other things. Originally, I aimed to incorporate all of these qualities into one logo and started by focusing on the most important elements.

My love for nature and self-growth, my "witchy side" if you will, served as my primary source of inspiration. Given my penchant for runes and tarot cards, I began researching symbols similar to them and came across Theban language, which I had unknowingly been using for years as its own form of communication.

The language's perfect fit with the two letter "t" in my logo led me to experiment with various Theban "t" placements, eventually resulting in a floral design with two Theban "t"s. I was amazed to see how this symbol could resemble a beetle, butterfly, mantaray, flower, and more, making it an "all-encompassing" symbol. Moreover, it appeared like an ink blot that people could interpret themselves, a feature that I found fascinating and likely to spark interesting conversations.

When searching for a name, I explored several "t" words, and the word "tantalizing" caught my eye. Its meaning, exciting one's senses or desires, aligns perfectly with what I aim to achieve with my brand- excitement, uniqueness, and fun. Furthermore, the name conveniently has two "t"s. The process of designing this logo was lengthy, but the gratification it brought was immense."
